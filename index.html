Bio

tiny specks are a dynamic indie and alternative rock trio that brings a unique and captivating sound to the music scene. The trio is lead by Nikki on bass and vocals, Brian on drums, and Vanessa on guitar.

The music is characterized by a fusion of indie rock vibes and alternative energy. Their innovative use of live loops adds an exciting layer to their performances, creating a truly immersive experience for their audience.

Drawing inspiration from iconic bands like Bikini Kill and Joy Division, tiny specks delivers a blend of raw energy, thought-provoking lyrics, and a distinctive sonic palette. Get ready to be swept away by the infectious energy and unique musical journey of tiny specks.
